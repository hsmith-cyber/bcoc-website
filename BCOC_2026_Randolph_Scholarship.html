Michael O. Randolph Scholarship

In Partnership with Waldorf University

The annual Michael O. Randolph Scholarship is presented to a BCOC member who exudes excellence in both their professional and personal life. This scholarship, offered in partnership with Waldorf University, reflects the BCOC's commitment to the continued growth and development of its members.

Waldorf University is a valued partner of the Black Chief Officers Committee. Together, we are committed to advancing educational opportunities and professional excellence within the fire service.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be an active member of the Black Chief Officers Committee (BCOC) for a minimum of two (2) consecutive years
  • Must be a currently paid member in good standing
  • Must demonstrate excellence in professional and personal endeavors

About the Scholarship

Named in honor of Michael O. Randolph, this scholarship embodies the spirit of leadership, service, and dedication that defines the BCOC. Recipients are selected based on their commitment to excellence, their contributions to the fire service profession, and their positive impact within their communities. The scholarship supports continued education through Waldorf University.
