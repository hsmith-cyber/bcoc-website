About Dr. Kim McNair

Dr. Kim McNair is a leadership strategist and the founder of Kim McNair Productions LLC, a firm dedicated to leadership development for public safety, fire, EMS, and municipal leaders. She works with chief officers, command staff, and emerging executives to strengthen the decision-making, resource stewardship, and leadership presence required to lead resilient teams in uncertain times.

At the center of her work is the KMP Mindset — a leadership framework built on three pillars that govern how executives lead under pressure, allocate constrained resources, and exercise authority with integrity:

K
Knowledge
The discipline of decision-making under pressure — building the situational, technical, and human awareness leaders need to act with clarity when it matters.
M
Money
The stewardship of resources — capital, personnel, and time — and the financial fluency that lets executives translate budgets into mission outcomes.
P
Power
The exercise of leadership presence and influence — using authority responsibly to align teams, navigate politics, and produce durable results.

Dr. McNair's approach is grounded in the lived realities of public-safety leadership. She helps organizations build command teams that hold their composure in high-stakes moments, manage the financial and operational levers that determine readiness, and cultivate the leadership presence required to inspire confidence inside and outside the organization. Her work is practical, evidence-informed, and culturally grounded — designed to translate directly into the daily decisions of the leaders who attend her sessions.

Core Focus Areas

  • Executive leadership development for fire, EMS, and public-safety chiefs
  • Decision-making frameworks for high-pressure command environments
  • Resource and budget stewardship for command staff
  • Leadership presence and the responsible exercise of authority
  • Building and sustaining resilient command teams
