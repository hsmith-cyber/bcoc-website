Endorsement Statement

Black Chief Officers Committee

The Black Chief Officers Committee proudly and enthusiastically endorses Fire Chief Corey Smedley for 2nd Vice President of the International Association of Fire Chiefs (IAFC).

Chief Smedley has consistently demonstrated a steadfast commitment to leadership, service, and the advancement of the fire and emergency services profession. His career reflects a deep understanding of the complexities facing today's fire service — ranging from operational excellence and workforce development to legislative advocacy and organizational culture.

He is a proven collaborator who brings people together across departments, disciplines, and communities. Chief Smedley's dedication to mentorship, recruitment, and professional development — particularly in creating pathways for the next generation of diverse leaders — aligns strongly with the values we uphold as a committee.

As a forward-thinking leader, he understands the importance of innovation while honoring the traditions of the fire service. His engagement with national programs, commitment to continuous learning, and ability to translate vision into action make him exceptionally well-prepared to serve the IAFC membership from day one.

We are confident that Chief Smedley will serve with integrity, inclusiveness, and purpose. His leadership will help strengthen the IAFC's mission and ensure it remains responsive to the evolving needs of the fire service.

For these reasons, the Black Chief Officers Committee is proud to stand in full support of Chief Corey Smedley for IAFC 2nd Vice President.
