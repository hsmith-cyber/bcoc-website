Elections Committee

Resolution 2026 · 03 — April 23, 2026
Elections Committee
Administers the 2026 election process under Article V of the BCOC Bylaws — receiving candidate submissions, verifying eligibility, coordinating ballots, ensuring the integrity of the balloting process, and certifying official results.
